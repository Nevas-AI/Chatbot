## What is A365 AI?

A365 AI is a **custom-built, intelligent chatbot** designed exclusively for **Ascent Innovation**. It lives on your website and provides instant, accurate responses to visitors' questions — 24/7, without any manual intervention.

> **Think of it as a tireless team member** who knows everything about your business and is always available to help customers.

---

## Key Features

### 🧠 AI-Powered Knowledge Base
- Automatically learns from your website content (ascent365.com)
- Understands **ERP, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Business Central**, and all your service offerings
- Provides context-aware, relevant answers — not generic chatbot responses

### ⚡ Real-Time Responses
- Instant replies to visitor questions
- Streaming responses for a natural, conversational feel
- Smart follow-up suggestions (quick-reply buttons)

### 🎨 Fully Branded Experience
- **Your company colors**, logo, and branding
- Custom bot name (**A365 AI**)
- Personalized welcome messages and greetings
- Seamless integration into your existing website design

### 📧 Automatic Lead Capture
- Collects visitor contact information naturally during conversations
- Sends lead notifications directly to your team's email
- Captures name, email, phone, and company details

### 🔄 Auto-Updating Knowledge
- Periodically re-scans your website for new content
- Always up-to-date with your latest services, blog posts, and pages
- No manual updates needed

## How It Works

```
 ┌─────────────────┐     ┌──────────────────┐     ┌─────────────────┐
 │   Website        │────▶│   A365 AI Engine  │────▶│  Your Knowledge │
 │   Visitor asks   │     │   Processes the   │     │  Base (1000+    │
 │   a question     │     │   question        │     │  data points)   │
 └─────────────────┘     └──────────────────┘     └─────────────────┘
                                  │
                                  ▼
                          ┌──────────────────┐
                          │  Delivers smart,  │
                          │  accurate answer  │
                          │  in real-time     │
                          └──────────────────┘
```

1. **Visitor** lands on any page of ascent365.com
2. **Chatbot widget** appears in the corner — ready to help
3. **Visitor asks** a question (e.g., "What is Business Central?")
4. **AI Engine** searches your knowledge base for the best answer
5. **Response** is delivered instantly with relevant information

## What Visitors See

When a visitor lands on your website:

1. A **branded chat button** appears in the bottom-right corner
2. Clicking it opens a sleek chat window with your **A365 logo** and colors
3. A **welcome message** greets them with quick-action buttons:
   - "What is Business Central?"
   - "ERP Implementation"
   - "Request a Demo"
   - "Contact Us"
4. They can type any question and get an **instant, intelligent response**
5. Contact details are **automatically captured** and sent to your team
